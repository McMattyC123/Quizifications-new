Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

iOS: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Quizifications → Cancel
Android: Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → Quizifications → Cancel

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Please contact them directly for refund requests.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove all your data.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please email us with a description of the bug, what device you're using, and steps to reproduce it. Screenshots help!

Can I use Quizifications for my school or organization?

We'd love to discuss group or educational pricing. Email us at matt@quizifications.com.
